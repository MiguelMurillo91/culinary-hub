Garlic Shrimp and Broccoli

Savor the perfect harmony of flavors with our Garlic Shrimp and Broccoli—an exquisite blend of succulent shrimp, vibrant broccoli, and aromatic garlic. Quickly sautéed to perfection, this dish captures the essence of freshness with a hint of zesty lemon and a sprinkle of parsley. Elevate your dinner with this delightful combination that's as easy to make as it is delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3 cups broccoli florets
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • Cooked rice or noodles for serving

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add shrimp to the skillet, season with salt and black pepper, and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side or until they turn pink and opaque. Remove shrimp from the skillet and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add the remaining 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
  4. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes (if using) to the skillet. Sauté for 1-2 minutes until garlic is fragrant.
  5. Add broccoli florets to the skillet and sauté for 3-4 minutes or until they are crisp-tender.
  6. Return the cooked shrimp to the skillet, add lemon juice, and toss everything together. Cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  7. Sprinkle chopped fresh parsley over the garlic shrimp and broccoli.
  8. Serve the Garlic Shrimp and Broccoli over cooked rice or noodles.

Enjoy this quick and flavorful dish that combines succulent shrimp, vibrant broccoli, and the aromatic richness of garlic!
