Lentils Salad

Elevate your lunch our vibrant Lentil Salad featuring wholesome green or brown lentils, crisp cucumber, and aromatic cilantro. Drizzled with a zesty olive oil and red wine vinegar dressing, this refreshing salad is a harmonious blend of flavors and textures. Quick to prepare and bursting with nutrition, it's a delightful addition to your repertoire of healthy meals.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dry green or brown lentils
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: feta cheese for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the lentils under cold water. In a saucepan, combine the lentils and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for about 20-25 minutes or until lentils are tender but still hold their shape.
  2. Drain any excess water from the cooked lentils and let them cool.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooked lentils, diced cucumber, chopped cilantro, and finely chopped red onion.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil and red wine vinegar. Pour the dressing over the lentil mixture and toss to coat.
  5. Season the salad with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Optional: Garnish with crumbled feta cheese for an extra layer of flavor.
  7. Chill the lentil salad in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving.

Enjoy your refreshing Lentil Salad with Cilantro and Cucumber as a light and nutritious dish!
