Overnight Oats

Revitalize your mornings with our Overnight Oats featuring a harmonious blend of creamy oats, Greek yogurt, and chia seeds. Sweetened with a hint of honey and infused with vanilla, this wholesome base transforms overnight. Awake to a symphony of flavors by topping it with succulent raspberries, plump blueberries, and a delightful crunch of sliced almonds. A delightful, nutritious start awaits!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk (dairy or plant-based) 1 tablespoon chia
  • seeds 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A handful of raspberries
  • Ahandful of blueberries
  • Sliced almonds for topping

Instructions

  1. In a jar or container, combine rolled oats, Greek yogurt, milk, chia seeds, honey (if using), and vanilla extract. Mix well.
  2. Seal the jar or container and refrigerate overnight or for at least 4 hours to allow the oats to absorb the liquid.
  3. In the morning, give the oats a good stir.
  4. Top the overnight oats with fresh raspberries, blueberries, and a generous sprinkle of sliced almonds.

Feel free to customize this recipe by adding your favorite toppings like shredded coconut, a drizzle of nut butter, or a sprinkle of cinnamon. It's a versatile and convenient breakfast option!
