Spaghetti with Tomato and Basil

Experience the timeless simplicity of our Spaghetti with Tomato and Basil—a classic Italian dish that captures the essence of fresh ingredients. Al dente spaghetti dances in a vibrant tomato sauce, infused with the aromatic allure of minced garlic and crowned with torn basil leaves. Garnish with Parmesan for a perfect balance of flavors in every comforting forkful.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz (about 225g) spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves, torn
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions in a large pot of salted boiling water. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes and red pepper flakes (if using). Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Simmer for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet, tossing to coat the pasta in the tomato sauce.
  5. Remove from heat and sprinkle torn fresh basil leaves over the pasta.
  6. Serve the Spaghetti with Tomato and Basil hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese.

Enjoy this simple and flavorful dish that celebrates the vibrant combination of ripe tomatoes and fragrant basil!
