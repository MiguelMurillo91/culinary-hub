Baked Salmon with Lemon and Dill

Elevate your dining experience with our Baked Salmon adorned with zesty lemon and aromatic dill. Each succulent fillet is bathed in a harmonious blend of olive oil, garlic, and Dijon mustard, creating a burst of flavor with each bite. The result is a dish that's not only delicious but also effortlessly elegant, perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet. Season with salt, black pepper, dried oregano, garlic powder, lemon zest, and drizzle with olive oil. Squeeze lemon juice over the fillets.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven for 15-20 minutes or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily.
  4. In a large bowl, combine cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red bell pepper, red onion, Kalamata olives, feta cheese, and fresh parsley.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together extra virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, salt, and black pepper. Pour the dressing over the Greek salad and toss gently to combine.
  6. Place the baked salmon fillets on top of the Greek salad.
  7. Serve the Greek Salad with Salmon immediately, and enjoy this flavorful and nutritious meal!

This Greek Salad with Salmon is a perfect blend of vibrant colors and flavors, offering a taste of the Mediterranean in every bite.
