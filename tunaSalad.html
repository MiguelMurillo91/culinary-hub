Mediterranean Tuna Salad

Transport your taste buds to the shores of the Mediterranean with our vibrant Tuna Salad. Succulent tuna meets a melody of cherry tomatoes, crisp cucumber, Kalamata olives, and crumbled feta, all tossed in a zesty olive oil and red wine vinegar dressing. Bursting with fresh flavors, this salad is a wholesome celebration of Mediterranean culinary delights that's both satisfying and nourishing.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (5 oz each) tuna, drained
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup Kalamata olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for serving (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine drained tuna, cherry tomatoes, diced cucumber, chopped red onion, sliced Kalamata olives, crumbled feta cheese, and chopped fresh parsley.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together extra virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the tuna mixture and toss gently to coat all ingredients.
  4. Chill the Mediterranean Tuna Salad in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  5. Before serving, give the salad a final toss and adjust salt and pepper if needed.
  6. Optionally, serve with lemon wedges for a burst of citrus freshness.

Enjoy this light and flavorful Mediterranean Tuna Salad as a satisfying and healthy meal!
