Banana Pancakes

Embark on a delightful breakfast journey with our Banana Pancakes recipe. Light, fluffy, and infused with the natural sweetness of ripe bananas, these pancakes are a morning treat. Perfectly paired with your favorite toppings, from a drizzle of maple syrup to fresh slices of banana, they offer a delicious start to your day that's both comforting and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • Cooking spray or additional butter for greasing the pan

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, mash the ripe banana.
  3. Add the buttermilk, egg, and melted butter to the mashed banana. Mix well.
  4. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Do not overmix; it's okay if there are lumps.
  5. Heat a griddle or non-stick skillet over medium heat. Lightly grease with cooking spray or butter.
  6. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle for each pancake.
  7. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until the other side is golden brown.
  8. Repeat with the remaining batter.
  9. Serve the banana pancakes warm with your favorite toppings such as maple syrup, sliced bananas, or a sprinkle of chopped nuts.

Enjoy your delicious and fluffy Banana Pancakes!
