Chicken and Quinoa Salad

Delight your taste buds with our Chicken and Quinoa Salad, a harmonious blend of protein-packed quinoa, succulent grilled chicken, and vibrant vegetables. Topped with juicy cherry tomatoes and crumbled feta, it's a burst of freshness in every bite. Drizzled with a zesty balsamic vinaigrette, this wholesome salad is a delicious and nutritious meal for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa
  • 2 cups water or chicken broth
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

Instructions

  1. Rinse quinoa under cold water. In a saucepan, combine quinoa and water or broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until quinoa is cooked and water is absorbed. Let it cool.
  2. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium-high heat. Cook chicken for 6-8 minutes per side or until cooked through. Let it rest for a few minutes, then slice.
  3. In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, sliced chicken, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, feta cheese, and parsley.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, balsamic vinegar, Dijon mustard, salt, and pepper. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  5. Top the salad with additional cherry tomatoes.
  6. Serve the Chicken and Quinoa Salad chilled or at room temperature.

Enjoy this flavorful and nutritious Chicken and Quinoa Salad with the burst of freshness from cherry tomatoes!
