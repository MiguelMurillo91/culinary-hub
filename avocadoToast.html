Avocado Toast

Indulge in our delectable Avocado Toast with Nuts and Cream Cheese recipeâ€”a symphony of creamy avocado, smooth cream cheese, and crunchy nuts atop crispy whole-grain bread. Elevate your breakfast or brunch with this simple, yet sensational, dish. Add a drizzle of honey for a delightful touch of sweetness. Let's dive in!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of whole-grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1/4 cup cream cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped mixed nuts (e.g., walnuts, almonds)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: a drizzle of honey for sweetness

Instructions

  1. Toast the whole-grain bread slices until they are crispy.
  2. While the bread is toasting, cut the ripe avocado in half and remove the pit. Scoop the avocado flesh into a bowl.
  3. Mash the avocado with a fork and season it with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  4. Once the toast is ready, spread the mashed avocado evenly on each slice.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix the cream cheese until it's smooth and creamy.
  6. Spread a generous layer of cream cheese on top of the avocado.
  7. Sprinkle the chopped nuts over the cream cheese.
  8. If you'd like a touch of sweetness, drizzle a little honey over the nuts.
  9. Your Avocado Toast with Nuts and Cream Cheese is ready to enjoy! It's a perfect balance of creamy, crunchy, and savory flavors.

Feel free to customize this recipe by adding additional toppings like cherry tomatoes, a poached egg, or a sprinkle of red pepper flakes for some extra kick.
